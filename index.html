How rates & columns are calculated
Exchange rates

Each month uses the EUR→INR mid-market rate recorded on the 10th of that specific month. Rates are hardcoded — no live API is used, so values are stable and auditable.

Sources: poundsterlinglive.com (Feb 2026) and Xe.com (Mar 2026 onwards). Future months show Rate TBD until the 10th of that month passes.

Column definitions
Net Remaining
Amount due minus amount paid for that specific month

Data & sync

All payment entries are stored in Firebase Realtime Database and sync live between both devices. When your friend logs a payment in India, your dashboard updates within seconds — no refresh needed.

The Firebase apiKey in the source is a project identifier, not a secret. Security is enforced by database rules that restrict access to the loandata path only.
